Spinach, Tofu & Sesame Stir-Fry

By: Martha Rose Shulman

Yield: 3 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon canola oil
  • ½ pound tofu, cut in small dice
  • 1 large garlic clove, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated or minced fresh ginger
  • ¼ teaspoon red chili flakes
  • Soy sauce to taste
  • 16-ounce bag baby spinach, rinsed
  • 2 tablespoons toasted sesame seeds
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil

Preparation

Step 1

Heat the canola oil over medium-high heat in a large nonstick skillet or wok, and add the tofu. Stir-fry until the tofu is lightly golden, about 3–5 minutes. Add garlic, ginger, and chili flakes, and cook for 1 minute until fragrant. Add soy sauce to taste, then toss in the spinach and stir-fry until wilted. Mix in sesame seeds, and season again with soy sauce if needed.

Step 2

Use tongs to transfer the mixture to a serving bowl, leaving excess liquid behind. Drizzle with sesame oil and serve warm with rice, noodles, or as a filling for pita bread.

Tip

This dish is best made fresh, but you can prep the ingredients earlier for a quick stir-fry at mealtime.
